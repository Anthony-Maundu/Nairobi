Welcome to Nairobi

Administrative Function

Nairobi is the capital city of Kenya and serves as the administrative center of the country. It hosts the main government offices, including the State House and Parliament.

Socio-Economic Function

Nairobi is a vibrant social and economic hub. It is known for its diverse population, cultural institutions, and as the location of many international businesses and organizations.

Religious Function

The city is a melting pot of various religions, with numerous churches, mosques, temples, and other places of worship reflecting the diversity of its inhabitants.

Political Function

As the political heart of Kenya, Nairobi is home to the country's key political institutions, including the Presidency, Parliament, and the Supreme Court.

Economic Function

Nairobi is a major economic hub in Africa, housing the Nairobi Securities Exchange (NSE) and numerous multinational companies. It is a leading city in finance, trade, and technology in the region.

Geological Function

Nairobi sits on the Nairobi River and is located at an altitude of about 1,795 meters above sea level. The city's geology includes volcanic rocks and sedimentary layers, influencing its topography and natural resources.
